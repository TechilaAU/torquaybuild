About Us

Torquay's local trade and retail building supplies store — stocking everything your project needs.

Our Story

Built for the
Surf Coast

Torquay Building Supplies was established to fill a simple gap — a proper local building supplies yard right here on the Surf Coast. No more driving to Geelong for a bag of cement or a roll of agi pipe.

We stock over 40 product lines to serve both trade and DIY customers. Whether you're a concretor laying a driveway, a landscaper building a retaining wall, or a homeowner wanting to tidy up the backyard — we've got what you need on the shelf, ready to go.

We're a local business, and that means you deal with real people who know the products and want to help your job go right.

Our Offer
